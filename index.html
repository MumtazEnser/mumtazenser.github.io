Mumtaz Enser

Philosophy of AI, Responsibility, and Normative Structures

Authority Illusion

Authority appears where decisions are not actually made.

“Authority does not reside where it appears.”

Ethical Withdrawal

Responsibility is not denied — it is deferred.

“I left the decision to the system.”

Protean Responsibility

Responsibility does not disappear — it shifts.

“Responsibility is never absent, only redistributed.”

Algorithms Do Not Decide

Outputs are mistaken for decisions.

“A system can produce an output. It cannot decide.”
